Homemade Hoagie

Description

The hoagie may seem too simple to be called a recipe, but there are many little things that can turn a passable sandwich into a truly palatable one! Why do you think the deli's sandwiches taste so much better than the ones you make at home? With the right tricks, those mouth-watering deli sandwiches can become a convenient part of your daily arsenal!

Ingredients

  • Baguette
  • Several sandwich meats of choice (e.g. Salami, pepperoni, turkey)
  • Cheese of choice (e.g. pepperjack)
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Sliced tomato (optional)
  • Honey Mustard
  • Vinegar (e.g. white vinegar)
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice the baguette open
  2. Add honey mustard to bottom and top halves of baguette.
  3. Add vingear to bottom and top halves of baguette.
  4. (Optional) If you like, mix some vigar with olive oil in a seperate bowl to make a vinegarette. Then, you can mix some salt into that vinegarette and apply that to your sandwich in place of vinegar.
  5. Add meat slices onto bottom half of baguette.
  6. Salt meat slices (lightly).
  7. Add cheese on top of meat slices. (The sticky cheese is the bond between the "meat layer" with the "vegetable layer.")
  8. Add tomato slices (optional).
  9. Add iceberg lettuce.
  10. Close baguette firmly.
  11. DONE! Now, you can pop your baguette in the toaster for extra crispiness, wrap it in wax paper to let the flavors/juices squeeze in more, or just eat it right now!
